Subscribing

The modules-interest mailing list is hosted at SourceForge using Mailman. The web interface where you can subscribe is perhaps your best bet, but it's not the only method.

You can also subscribe to the modules mailing list by sending an email to "modules-interest-request@lists.sourceforge.net" with the text "subscribe" in the subject and/or body of the message.

In order to unsubscribe, change the word "subscribe" in the above instruction to "unsubscribe" and send the message.

Mailing List Administration

The administrative interface for changing your options is the same as the link above for subscribing. Once you have subscribed, you will be sent an email with your password and some links that give you direct access to the administrative interface; remember to save that email for future reference.

Mailing List Archive
